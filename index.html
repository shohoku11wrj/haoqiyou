Sunday Road Ride: Counter-clockwise IBM

Hello team, This Sunday, we will head to IBM from the counter clockwise direction, ascending from the north entrance. Before beginning the ascent, we will stop for bathroom and water refill at the Almaden Quicksilver Park Mockingbird Hill entrance. Upon completing the climb, we will do a celebratory lap around the IBM campus before beginning the descent down the north entrance (same side we came up) and heading back to Mike’s Bike’s in Los Gatos. The route is 30 miles with 2,200 ft of elevation gain. We will meet at Mike’s Bike’s in Los Gatos and 8:30 am and wheels will roll at 8:35 am sharp. This is a no-drop ride. I will do my best to keep the group together. I hope to see you there!
