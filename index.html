Saturday Tempo Ride

This is a 65 mile ride with 2.5k-3k of elevation which feels like less. We'll start at Mike's and ride to the coffee shop in Morgan Hill by Bike Therapy, and the brew pub if that's your thing. For the bigger stronger non climbers this is probably a Z2/Z3 mostly ride. We can stop at Calero for bathrooms and water in both directions if need be. I intend to have coffee and a pastry in morgan Hill which should take us 2-2.25 hours to reach, and the stop will be 30-40 minutes (not so long that you totally cool off). The ride home is probably 1.75 hours. This is sort of a classic base miles ride with a little more pep than you'd probably do in February, with chances to pace line, go hard up a few mini-climbs, or just draft the whole way if that suits you. You can even hit some sprint signs, just regroup after that. Route is here: https://ridewithgps.com/routes/52796073
